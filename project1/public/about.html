About Drop Bears

Drop Bears are a mythical Australian animal often described as large, vicious koala-like creatures. They are said to inhabit the treetops of the Australian bush, waiting for unsuspecting prey to pass below. Though considered a part of Australian folklore, many tourists are still warned about the dangerous Drop Bear during their visits.

The legend of the Drop Bear claims that these creatures drop from trees to attack and eat their prey. According to the story, they are carnivorous and have sharp teeth and claws, capable of taking down large animals, including humans. Many locals humorously use the tale to scare tourists, adding to the Drop Bear's infamous reputation.

Although there is no scientific evidence to support the existence of Drop Bears, the legend persists in popular culture. Some tourists have reported sightings, though these are generally dismissed as pranks. The Drop Bear remains one of Australia's most entertaining and enduring myths, with its story being passed down through generations.
